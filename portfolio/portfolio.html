Product Review Series

October, 2020

Video format

Product Review is a series of videos in which I go over different digital products analyzing their main features, as well as the value they may offer to their users.

Although this series of videos is not yet share with public audiences, you gan review one of these videos on the Forest App Product Review YouTube link, English subtitules have been added.

I like to start the process with a simple storyboard in which I capture the main parts of the video idea, then I moved into the script creation with different notes that must be followed during the recording session. I always try to extra shots just in case additional footage is required, as well as to immediately back up all of the video and voice recording, as sometimes locations cannot be accessed once again for additional recording. Editing is the one of the most time consuming parts as the one in which I can spend a whole day sitting just to get the result expected. Once the final version is approved, I export with the required settings depending of the platform it would be hosted in.
