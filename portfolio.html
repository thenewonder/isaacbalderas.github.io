Product Review Series

October, 2020

Video format

Product Review is a series of videos in which I go over different digital products analyzing their main features, as well as the value they may offer to their users.

Although this series of videos is not yet share with public audiences, you can review one of these videos on the Forest App Product Review YouTube link, English subtitules have been added.

Regional Product Trainings

February 19, 2019

As part of the go-to-market plan of any product it is essential that the people involved in commercial, customer, and operation areas are aware of the general overview of a product, as well as its main features. I had experience providing product trainings in different countries, including Mexico, Brazil, Peru, and other latin american markets.

On the next link, you can download a sample of the Compliance Engagement Manual that was provided to the commercial teams for further reading.

Along with the on-site training, different internal materials and marketing collaterals were created including brochures, product presentations, pitch decks, user manuals, marketing and walkthrough videos, email templates, and training presentations.
