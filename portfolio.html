DUNS Registered User Manuals & Video Guides

Development of Compliance Traffic Light feature

January 2020

After interviewing with different costumers, and gathering their feedback, we identified a lack of understanding on the results provided by the tool. We decided to create a simple traffic light based on the data found to easily indicate the costumer the risk level of the applicant.

On the next YouTube link, you can watch the video created to introduce this new feature with internal teams and users Compliance Watch Traffic Light as well as the New Features Watch Newsletter (English) with this and other features released during this sprint

I started this assessment based on comments from the commercial teams when approaching costumers, and after reviewing the consumption data from these costumers and validating that it was low compared to other costumers, I decided to schedule a set of interviews with these costumers to try to understand the reasons behind. After several sessions, I moved on with the general process of a new development, to then test this feature with the original costumers that reported some issues on the data understanding. Regional Product Trainings

February 2019

As part of the go-to-market plan of any product it is essential that the people involved in commercial, customer, and operation areas are aware of the general overview of a product, as well as its main features. I had experience providing product trainings in different countries, including Mexico, Brazil, Peru, and other Latin American markets.

On the next link, you can download a sample of the Compliance Engagement Manual that was provided to the commercial teams for further reading, as well as one of the Brochures (Spanish), and Training Decks (English) provided.

Along with the on-site training, different internal materials and marketing collaterals were created including brochures, product presentations, pitch decks, user manuals, marketing and walkthrough videos, email templates, and training presentations. The training design took me around three months, in which I first developed the initial sketching of the documents, define the audiences, contact marketing agencies, aligned with design teams, prepare the agenda proposed to different country managers, and execute the plan defined and the beginning of the process. In order to get feedback about the training quality, at the of each session, an anonymous survey was provides, as well as different quizzes that evaluate the knowledge of the teams in the training provided.
